🔌 Stratum Connection Info

How to connect your miners to the pool. Each coin has three stratum ports:

V1 (TCP) Standard stratum — works with all miners V2 (Binary) Stratum V2 encrypted protocol — V2-compatible miners TLS (SSL) Encrypted V1 stratum — industrial ASICs only (see note below)

TLS Compatibility Notice

TLS stratum ports use a self-signed certificate (required for private IP pools). This works with industrial ASICs (Antminer, Whatsminer, Avalon, BraiinsOS) which skip certificate verification.

ESP32-based miners (BitAxe, NerdQAxe, NMaxe, QAxe, Lucky Miner, Jingle Miner, Zyber) cannot use TLS ports — their mbedTLS firmware strictly verifies certificates and will reject self-signed certs. Use the standard V1 (TCP) port instead and disable "Encrypted connection (TLS)" in your device settings.
